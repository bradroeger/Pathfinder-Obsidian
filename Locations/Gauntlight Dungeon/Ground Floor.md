### Important Notes

We heard voices speaking in a horrible language called [[Sakvoth]].
The place is filled with Gremlin fey called [[Mitfits]].
There was a teleporter here but the silver has been stolen from the seal.
[[Boss Skrawng]] appeared and spoke Common to us.

We found a secret passage into the basement.
The secret room led to a dungeon where we found multiple horrible creatures that could not see, called [[Mortlock]]s.

Found a haunt of betrayed kobolds.
Huge bloodstain in the middle of the tower.
[[Lighthouse]]

Appears to be able to shoot a beam that raises the dead.
Also appears to be able to teleport in horrible monsters.
A sign of [[Belcorra Haruvex]]'s return?

We found a thief trapped in a secret area. He was bludgeoned to death, along with several items on his person:

healing potion minor elixir A15 silver ring (5gp) 4x daggers rapier (magical?)

Found a religious symbol of a dark god called [[Nhimbaloth]].
The shrine has a connection to a woman we saw in a portrait. I believe this is [[Belcorra Haruvex]].

The blood had an image of [[Belcorra Haruvex]] — hurt, injured, and stabbed. Betrayed by someone, and radiating so much anger.

Perhaps at night the blood will manifest into a great and terrible horror.
