The starting town.
Celebrating [[Founder's Day]].
Founded in 4242 AR.
Logging town.

![[Otari Map.png]]

Giant's Wheel - Lumber wheel.
[[Menheems manor]] - Town Mayor. One wing is closed off — too big — and one wing is the town museum.
[[Wrin's Wonders]] - Supported our quest giver.
[[Galintines Deliveries]] - Post service; occasionally requires help delivering post.
[[Blades for Glades]] - Blacksmith shop, weapons and armour.
[[Otari Garrison]] - Guard station, fire fighters, and barristers.
[[Odd Stories]] - Run by a wizard/charlatan providing spell work. Owns a selection of spells, can cast others, and sells scrolls of the arcane and occult.
[[Bespoke and Rat]] - Rattler's shop; provides rats.
[[Farmers Guild]] - Rooms free of charge for farmers, or available to rent if needed.
[[Crow's Casks]] - Strange liquids / alchemists.
[[Otari Market]] - Log cabin trading post, gathering place for locals; general goods and farmers' market.
[[Crooks Nook]] - Pub with good food but a rough reputation; has lodging. Enjoyed by the shadier types.
[[Otari Fishery]] - Deals in the fish that comes in; gathering place for games and entertainment.
[[Rowdy Rockfish]] - Quaint tavern.
[[Dawnflower Library]] - Church to Sarenrae, but other denominations are available within the stacks. Fiction to history and mostly tasteful erotica.
[[Otari Graveyard]] - Rarely visited because it's haunted and burials have gone out of fashion.
[[Stonering Pond]] - 2 dozen standing stones; druids?
[[Inkleholtz Manor]] - Owns the lumber mill.
[[Churtil Manor]] - Rival company.
[[Captain of the Guard]] - Longstanding; doing his job but not well liked as a result.
Previous Captain Trusk Gainley - Died because of a bet involving raspberry wine.
The sister of the cleric of the temple stole expensive books.
